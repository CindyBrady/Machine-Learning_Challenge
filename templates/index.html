Analysis of NBA Data (Seasons: 2012/2013 - 2018/2019)

We selected our data source from kaggle.com. Our dataset consisted of raw scores, vegas betting line stats from both the regular and post seasons. We have used machine learning to validate the efficacy on a team's chances of making the playoffs based on several key offensive statics.

Machine Learning

Through Logistic Regression and Random Forest models we were able to review the 2012-2019 NBA seasons and predict which teams would go to the Playoffs. To isolate which factors were most influential for teams to make it to the playoff rounds, we implemented Model Based Feature Importance. We created visualizations in Tableau and Power BI to further analyze the identified key influencers and depict the accuracy of the models.
